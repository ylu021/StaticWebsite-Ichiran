What's Ichiran?

Ichiran (一蘭) is a Japanese ramen chain from Fukuoka. Found in 1993, it had further expanded in Japan and established branches in Hong Kong. In 2010 Ichiran USA has opened its Production Factory on 386 Johnson Ave Brooklyn, New York. Plans are to open shop in Summer of 2016.

Welcome to Ichiran!

At Ichiran we don’t diversify our menu, but concentrate only on Tonkotsu Ramen. Our mission is to deepen the flavor of every bowl, each one of them involving the work of over 40 specialists.

The safety and satisfaction of our customers is our priority. That is why we only use the highest-in-quality products for the elaboration of our ramen. Our professionals are masters of the complicated techniques involved in ramen making, and they always pursue a higher level of exquisiteness for our customers.
