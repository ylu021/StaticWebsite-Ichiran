What's Ichiran?

Ichiran (一蘭) is a Japanese ramen chain from Fukuoka. Found in 1993, it had further expanded in Japan and established branches in Hong Kong. In 2010 Ichiran USA has opened its Production Factory on 386 Johnson Ave Brooklyn, New York. Plans are to open shop in Summer of 2016.

What is "Classic" Tonkotsu Ramen？

Our Classic Tonkotsu Ramen began in 1960 with the foundation of ICHIRAN. A perfect bowl of 100% white cloudy pork broth in its purest form, straight delicate noodles and Hiden no Tare (Spicy Red Sauce).

Only Tonkotsu Ramen

Since 1960, ICHIRAN has been solely committed to serving only the highest quality tonkotsu ramen. This and bringing authentic flavors to your palette is our goal at ICHIRAN. Behind each and every bowl, our team of dedicated experts continue to hone their skills and innovate to craft you the perfect bowl of the Classic Tonkotsu Ramen.

Our Freshly Made Noodles
